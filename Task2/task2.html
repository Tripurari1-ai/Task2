Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes and stir for 1 minute.
  4. Toss in the cooked pasta and mix well.
  5. Season with salt, garnish with parsley, and serve with Parmesan.
